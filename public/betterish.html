Founder

I founded Betterish JLPT because as I was preparing for the N2 level of the JLPT (Japanese Language Proficiency Test) in 2013, I realized that Japanese self-study resources were painfully inadequate. Though I passed, I wanted to help others learn from my (many) mistakes.

At Betterish, I work as the editor, main writer and am responsible for the site maintenance and design
