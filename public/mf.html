Market Finder

Co-Creator

Good cupcakes are hard to find. That's why for our final project at Wyncode my partner and I created Market Finder, a site that makes it easy for customers to find and follow their favorite vendors at South Florida farmer's markets.

Responsibilities:

  • Site design
  • Rails model design
  • Responsive design
  • Feature implementation:
    • Authentication & account creation
    • Date scheduler
    • Vendor profiles
    • Picture upload
    • Follow a vendor

Tools & Technologies:

  • Heroku

  • Amazon S3

  • Adobe Creative Suite
